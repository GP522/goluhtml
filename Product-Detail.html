BOPP FILMS

Wrap Around
Label Films

GulfPack's BOPP films for wrap-around labels are used primarily in the food and beverage industry. These films are designed to wrap around a product's container completely, providing a seamless label that covers the entire circumference. They are commonly used for bottles, jars, cans, and other similar packaging formats.
